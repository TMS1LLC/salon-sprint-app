## Logo Concepts

### Concept 1: Deconstructed Female Sprinter Profile ⭐ PRIMARY CONCEPT
**Date Added:** December 23, 2025

**Description:**
- Female sprinter in profile/side view
- Deconstructed/geometric style (not realistic)
- Curly hair flowing/blowing back to show motion and speed
- Silhouette or line art style
- Emphasis on movement and energy

**Design Direction:**
- Modern, minimalist aesthetic
- Dynamic motion implied through hair movement
- Feminine energy (reflects target audience: salon professionals)
- Hair as focal point (directly relates to salon industry)
- Could be abstract/geometric for versatility

**Color Palette Ideas:**
- Primary: Indigo (#4F46E5) - professional, trustworthy
- Accent: Purple/magenta gradient for hair - energy, creativity
- Alternative: Coral/pink accents - warmth, approachability

**Technical Considerations:**
- Scalable (must work at small sizes for app icon)
- Recognizable in monochrome (for printing, favicons)
- Hair details visible even when reduced
- Clean lines for digital reproduction

**Inspiration Keywords:**
- Motion
- Speed
- Wind
- Energy
- Feminine strength
- Professional athlete aesthetic
- Salon industry (hair-focused)

**Potential Variations:**
1. **Full Profile Version:** Complete sprinter silhouette with flowing hair
2. **Hair Focus Version:** Just the hair and minimal head outline
3. **Icon Version:** Highly simplified for app icon (16x16 to 512x512)
4. **Wordmark Version:** Sprinter integrated with "Salon Sprint" text

**Why This Concept Works:**
- ✅ Directly communicates "sprint" (speed, urgency)
- ✅ Hair element connects to salon industry
- ✅ Feminine appeal matches target demographic (80%+ female stylists)
- ✅ Dynamic/energetic matches brand personality
- ✅ Unique - not generic delivery truck icon
- ✅ Memorable and distinctive
- ✅ Works across all media (app, website, business cards, vehicle wraps)

**Next Steps:**
- [ ] Sketch rough concepts (3-5 variations)
- [ ] Test at different sizes (1024px down to 32px)
- [ ] Create digital mockups in vector format (Adobe Illustrator/Figma)
- [ ] Test on colored backgrounds (light, dark, gradient)
- [ ] A/B test with target audience (stylists)

## Typography

### Font Recommendations

**Primary (Logo & Headers):**
- **Option 1:** Montserrat (Bold/ExtraBold) - Modern, geometric, clean
- **Option 2:** Poppins (SemiBold/Bold) - Friendly, approachable
- **Option 3:** Inter (Bold) - Tech-forward, highly legible

**Body Text (App & Website):**
- Inter (Regular/Medium) - Excellent screen readability
- System fonts for app performance

**Tagline:**
- Lighter weight of primary font
- Uppercase or sentence case depending on context

## Competitive Visual Analysis

### What Competitors Use (To Avoid):
- **Generic delivery apps:** Truck/box icons (DoorDash, Uber Eats)
- **Salon apps:** Scissors, mirrors, generic beauty icons (GlossGenius)
- **Marketplace apps:** Shopping bags, carts

### Our Differentiation:
- **Sprinter with hair:** Unique to us, industry-specific
- **Motion/speed focus:** Emphasizes our key value prop
- **Feminine aesthetic:** Speaks to our core audience
- **Not a truck:** We're about speed and service, not just logistics
